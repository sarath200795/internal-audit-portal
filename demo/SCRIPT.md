## Full narration (plain text)

Welcome to Internal Audit Portal. ISO 45001 audit management — plan audits, log findings, and close CAPA on a live compliance dashboard.

In this short tutorial, we'll walk through how to use the app.

First, register your organization. The first person to sign up becomes the administrator.

Enter your details and a password, then create the organization.

Compliance dashboard. Open findings, overdue CAPAs, audit progress and closure rate — live.

Sites. Manage the facilities your audits are scheduled against.

Findings register. All findings by type (Major/Minor NC, OFI, Observation), filterable and searchable.

CAPA board. A Kanban board: Open → In Progress → Verified → Closed; closing a CAPA closes its finding.

Admin. Approve teammates and manage member roles and org settings.

Internal Audit — plan, find, close, prove.. Start by registering your organization.

That's a quick tour of Internal Audit Portal. Thanks for watching — get started by registering your organization.
